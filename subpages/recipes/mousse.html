Chocolate mousse

By Mondelez International

Chocolate mousse
(Source: Mondelez International)

Ingredients

(for 2 servings, 1/2 cup each):

  • 8 triangles (about 2-1/2 oz.)
  • 1-1/2 cups thawed COOL WHIP Whipped Topping
  • TOBLERONE Swiss Milk Chocolate with Honey and Almond Nougat

Preparation

1. MICROWAVE chocolate in medium microwaveable bowl on HIGH 1 min. or until melted. Cool 1 min.

2. ADD whipped topping; stir with wire whisk until well blended.

3. REFRIGERATE at least 5 min. Garnish with fresh berries and mint leaves, if desired.

Substitue

Prepare as directed, using COOL WHIP LITE Whipped Topping.

Variation - White Chocolate Mousse:

Prepare as directed, using TOBLERONE Swiss White Chocolate with Honey and Almond Nougat.
